Let's Play Connect 4!

How to Play:

  • This is a 2 player game: Red Player and Black Player.
  • Red player goes first.
  • Click on a token slot in the top row to select which column your token will fall.
  • Whichever player gets 4 of his/her tokens in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.
  • When a winner is identified, hit the red button to reset the game.
